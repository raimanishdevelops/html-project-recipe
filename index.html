Name of the dish is momo

Steps to Make Momo

  1. Prepare the dough with flour, water, and salt
  2. Knead until smooth and let it rest for 30 minutes
  3. Mix filling with minced meat, onions, and spices
  4. Roll dough and cut into small circles
  5. Fill each circle with the meat mixture
  6. Fold and pleat the edges to seal
  7. Steam in a steamer for 15-20 minutes until cooked
  8. Serve hot with sauce

Ingredients

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Water - 3/4 cup
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Minced meat - 1 pound
  • Onions - 2 medium, finely chopped
  • Ginger - 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Garlic - 1 tablespoon, minced
  • Green chilies - 2, finely chopped
  • Coriander - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin - 1/2 teaspoon
